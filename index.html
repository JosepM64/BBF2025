Barcelona Balboa Festival 2025

31 Oct - 2 Nov

About us

Welcome to another edition of the Barcelona Balboa Festival 2025, from October 31st to November 2nd.

The BBF is an international festival organized by BCNswing, a non-profit association, in collaboration with Gloria Garcia. As a non-profit organization, we strive to keep prices affordable while maintaining the highest quality standards, offering you exceptional classes with personalized attention from international teachers who use an innovative methodology centered on students.

You will experience the best live swing music at parties with non-stop dancing. No competitions! We try to maintain a good leader-follower balance to ensure maximum dancing opportunities for everyone.

There will also be time to enjoy the city and its attractions.

Venues

This year, all parties will once again take place at the Casino l'Aliança del Poblenou,  located in the Poblenou neighborhood of the Sant Martí district , close to Bogatell Beach .

  • Rambla del Poblenou, 42, Sant Martí, 08005 Barcelona

Casino Aliança del Poblenou

The Casino l'Aliança del Poblenou is a stunning historical theater nestled in the heart of Barcelona's Poblenou district. It's a popular destination for diverse events, ranging from captivating concerts and theatrical productions to energetic swing parties. The theater boasts a spacious dance floor, making it an ideal haven for Balboa enthusiasts.

The wooden floor provides an excellent surface for dancers, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience. Additionally, the high-quality sound system adds to the vibrant atmosphere, creating the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable evening of Balboa dancing.
